Lunifer Privacy Policy

Effective date: March 30, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Lunifer ("Lunifer," "we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, stores, and shares information when you use the Lunifer mobile app.

This policy includes the current contact and publisher details provided for Lunifer.

1. Who We Are

Lunifer is provided by Douglas Brown. If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or your information, contact us at luniferAlarm@outlook.com.

2. Information We Collect

Depending on how you use Lunifer, we may collect and process the following categories of information:

  • Account information, such as your email address and authentication identifiers when you sign in using email/password or Google Sign-In.
  • Profile and onboarding information, such as age range, lifestyle, wake days, calendar preference, sleep preference, morning routine duration, and commute preference.
  • Sleep and wellness-related information, such as inferred sleep history, sleep duration, sleep recommendations, and WHOOP sleep-related data if you choose to connect WHOOP.
  • Calendar information, if you grant access, including calendar events used to support scheduling features.
  • Location information, if you grant access, including manually entered home location details and any location-based data used for commute-related features.
  • Motion and device sensor information, if you grant access, used to infer sleep activity and app functionality.
  • Alarm and preferences data, such as selected sounds, snooze settings, notification settings, override times, and app configuration.
  • Technical and usage information, such as timestamps, device-generated event data, and diagnostic-style operational data needed to run the service.

3. WHOOP Integration

If you choose to connect your WHOOP account, Lunifer requests permission to access WHOOP data authorized by you through the WHOOP OAuth flow. Lunifer currently uses WHOOP data to obtain sleep-related recommendation information. WHOOP access tokens are handled through our backend services and are not intended to be exposed publicly.

4. How We Use Information

We may use information we collect to:

  • Provide, operate, maintain, and improve Lunifer.
  • Create and manage your account.
  • Generate wake-up recommendations, alarm scheduling features, sleep insights, and related app functionality.
  • Sync your data across devices or restore it after sign-in.
  • Provide WHOOP-connected features when you choose to connect WHOOP.
  • Provide customer support, investigate bugs, and protect the security and integrity of the app.
  • Comply with legal obligations and enforce our terms and policies.

5. Legal Bases for Processing

If you are in a jurisdiction that requires a legal basis for processing personal data, we rely on one or more of the following where applicable: your consent, performance of a contract with you, our legitimate interests in operating and improving the app, and compliance with legal obligations.

6. How We Share Information

We do not sell your personal information. We may share information in the following circumstances:

  • With service providers and infrastructure partners that help us operate the app, such as Firebase / Google Cloud and authentication providers.
  • With WHOOP, when you choose to connect your WHOOP account through their OAuth flow.
  • With Apple platform services when you use Apple-device permissions and system integrations.
  • When required by law, regulation, legal process, or to protect rights, safety, and security.
  • In connection with a merger, acquisition, financing, or sale of all or part of our business.

7. Data Storage and Retention

Data may be stored locally on your device and in cloud services we use to operate Lunifer. We retain information for as long as reasonably necessary to provide the app, fulfill the purposes described in this policy, comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce agreements.

8. Your Choices

  • You can choose whether to create an account and whether to connect WHOOP.
  • You can deny or revoke app permissions such as location, calendar, and motion access in your device settings.
  • You can sign out of the app.
  • You can request deletion of your account from within the app if that feature is available, or by contacting us.

9. Your Privacy Rights

Depending on where you live, you may have rights to access, correct, delete, or obtain a copy of your personal information, or to object to or restrict certain processing. To exercise these rights, contact us at luniferAlarm@outlook.com.

10. Security

We use reasonable administrative, technical, and organizational measures designed to protect information. However, no method of transmission over the internet or method of storage is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

11. Children

Lunifer is not intended for children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided personal information to us, contact us so we can review and take appropriate action.

12. International Transfers

Your information may be processed and stored in countries other than your own, including the United States, where privacy laws may differ from those in your jurisdiction.

13. Third-Party Services

Lunifer may rely on third-party services, including Firebase, Google Sign-In, Apple system frameworks, and WHOOP. Their handling of information is governed by their own privacy policies and terms.

14. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. If we make material changes, we may notify you by updating the effective date above, posting the revised policy, or using other appropriate means.
